Transformation of a Bodhisattva, Footsteps of an Ascetic Monk
◎ Compiled by Shi Hengchi 		　 　 　

一九七四年  西雅图

l974  Seattle

值此“世界和平聚会”，两位比丘，恒具及恒由，刚好完成第一次的三步一拜，抵达西雅图。另两位参与此会的比丘，恒观及恒空，正在实行七十二天的绝食。

The Gathering coincided with the arrival in the Seattle area of Bhikshus Heng Ju and Heng Yo, the two monks who executed the first Three-Steps-One-Bow pilgrimage. Two other monks, Bhikshus Heng Kuan and Heng Kung, joined the Gathering in the midst of their seventy-two-day fasts.
