A BIG STAR HAS FALLEN
◎ A talk by Venerable Bhante Dharmawara during the Memorial Ceremony for
the Venerable Master Hua on July 28,
1995 at the Sagely City of Ten Thousand Buddhas

The most venerable Mahatheras and Maha Upsakas and Upasikas: A big star has fallen. On account of that, we are gathering here to express our gratitude for the good that the big star has left behind. Now the fall of the big star makes room for another star. I am quite sure we are thinking of the big star, and our love for the big star has not ended with his fall. But we have to understand that the fall of the big star has provided room for the second one, and I am sure the second one will not fail to perform his duty to replace the loss. I am sure we have much to gain by the new one. This is my understanding. Thank you so much for giving me time.
