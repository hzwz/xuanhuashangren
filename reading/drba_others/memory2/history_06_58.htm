In Memory of the Venerable Master Hsuan Hua

菩萨幻化，头陀行迹．释恒持整理
Transformation of a Bodhisattva, Footsteps of an Ascetic Monk
◎ Compiled by Shi Hengchi 		　 　 　

一九七六年  圣他克拉拉

l976  Santa Clara

上人的德行感动了美国青年，并逐渐地降伏了他们强烈的独立感及“自由”的观念，使他们乐意学习佛教的仪轨，如持念佛菩萨圣号。照片中，他们随着出家众绕佛。

The Master’s virtue moved young Americans, gently subduing their strong sense of independence and concept of “freedom,” so that they willingly learned simple Buddhist rituals such as reciting Buddha’s and Bodhisattva’s names. Shown here, they follow the monks and nuns in circumambulation.
