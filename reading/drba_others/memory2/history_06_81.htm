一九七九年  万佛圣城

l979  City of Ten Thousand Buddhas

十一月，上人于万佛宝殿为千手千眼观世音菩萨圣像开光。

November. The Master opening the light on the Thousand-Handed, Thousand-Eyed Guanyin Bodhisattva in the Hall of Ten Thousand Buddhas.

围绕着上人的是年轻的美国及亚洲僧众弟子：比丘恒佐、恒朝、恒无、恒贡、恒实及沙弥果尔。（从左到右）

 　

The Master is surrounded by young American and Asian disciple-monks: (from left to right) Bhikshus Heng Tso, Heng Chau, Heng Wu, Heng Gung, and Heng Sure, and Shramanera Guo Erh.
