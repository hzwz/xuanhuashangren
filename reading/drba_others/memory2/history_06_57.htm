l976  Santa Clara
Central Park: The Master (seated at far left) speaks to young Americans about “Energies for Peace.”

从照片中可以很明显地看出，上人与美国年轻人的缘份。不管上人走到哪里，他率直的谈吐、慈悲的温馨，还有跨越了语言及文化障碍的神奇沟通能力，会立刻吸引了这些年轻人。

The Master’s affinities with young Americans are evident in this photo. Wherever he went, young Americans were immediately attracted to his straight talk, his compassionate warmth, and his uncanny ability to communicate that transcended language and cultural barriers.
