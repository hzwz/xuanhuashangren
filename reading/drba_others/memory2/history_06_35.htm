千手千眼观世音菩萨

Thousand-Handed, Thousand-Eyed Guanyin Bodhisattva

这尊观世音菩萨圣像，目前安放在万佛圣城的万佛宝殿。在这之前，是供奉在金山禅寺。当时，这尊观世音圣像尚未贴金。

The Guanyin Bodhisattva image that now presides at the City of Ten Thousand Buddhas first graced Gold Mountain Dhyana Monastery. At that time, the Guanyin Bodhisattva image was not yet gilded.
