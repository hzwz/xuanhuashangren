修道行者莫自欺

Cultivators of the Way, do not cheat yourselves.

掩耳盗铃甚不宜

It won’t work to plug your ears while stealing a bell.

开花难结真实果

Barren blossoms cannot bear real fruit.

光阴空过太可惜

What a shame to leave precious time pass by vain!
